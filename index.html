How the Tech Works

This platform utilizes Large Language Models (LLMs). Specifically, we integrate with Google's Gemini architecture. When you submit a topic, our backend logic constructs a "System Prompt" that instructs the AI to behave as a professional tutor.


The AI predicts the most relevant key terms, definitions, and structural hierarchies associated with your topic to generate notes that are not just accurate, but pedagogically sound.
